Pozole Rojo (serves 6-8):
- 1 kg pork shoulder, cubed
- 1 can hominy (800g), drained
- 4 guajillo chiles, deseeded
- 2 ancho chiles, deseeded
- 1 onion, quartered
- 4 garlic cloves
- Salt, oregano, cumin

Cook pork in water with salt until tender (about 2 hours). Toast and soak chiles, then blend with garlic and onion. Strain sauce into the pot. Add hominy. Simmer 30 min. Serve with shredded cabbage, radish, oregano, lime, and tostadas.
