Hey! Here's that pasta recipe I was telling you about. It's super easy:

Creamy Garlic Pasta (serves 2):
- 200g spaghetti
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- 2 tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil for garnish

1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
3. Pour in heavy cream, stir, and let simmer for 3-4 minutes.
4. Add parmesan and stir until melted and smooth.
5. Toss in the pasta, adding pasta water as needed for consistency.
6. Season with salt and pepper. Top with fresh basil.
