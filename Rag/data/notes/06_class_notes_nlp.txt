Lecture notes — Computational Intelligence, Week 11 (NLP)

Key concepts:

Tokenization: splitting text into tokens (words, subwords, characters).
  - Word-level: simple but large vocabulary, can't handle unknown words
  - Subword (BPE): balance between vocab size and coverage
  - Tools: tiktoken (OpenAI), HuggingFace tokenizers

Bag of Words (BoW): represents text as word frequency vector
  - Loses word order
  - Sparse, high-dimensional
  - Good baseline for classification

TF-IDF: like BoW but weights by importance
  - TF = term frequency in document
  - IDF = inverse document frequency across corpus
  - Penalizes common words (the, is, a)

Next class: embeddings — dense vectors that capture semantic meaning!
